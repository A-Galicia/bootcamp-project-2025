On Lifting

Date: 11/9/2022

Lifting weights isn’t just for bodybuilders or athletes it’s one of the best things you can do for your body and mind. Whether your goal is to get stronger, feel better, or just move through life with more ease, strength training offers benefits that go far beyond the gym.

If you’re new to lifting, start simple. Focus on proper form and basic movements like squats, pushes, pulls, and hinges. Don’t be afraid to ask for help from a trainer or experienced lifter good technique is everything. Remember: progress takes time, but every rep counts. Be patient, be consistent, and enjoy the process of getting stronger.
